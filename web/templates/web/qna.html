Q&A

How can VerriBell forms be used in my country?

As privacy and data protection laws vary, please check with qualified legal counsel or your government with regards to what you can or cannot ask and any special conditions that must be observed.

Can VerriBell be used to create other kinds of declaration forms?

You can create a variety of declaration forms other than health declarations.

What kind of declaration formats can I create?

You may create text, audio, image or video declaration forms.

How long are VerriBell declaration records stored?

VerriBell declaration records are stored for three (3) weeks. You may change it if necessary.

Why only three (3) weeks?

VerriBell values users' data privacy rights and strives to store personal data for only as long as necessary, in adherence to data privacy principles.
